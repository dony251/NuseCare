WHO WE ARE

About NurseCare

NurseCare is a university frontend project that simulates a home nursing service platform — inspired by Uber.

🎯

Our Mission

To make professional home nursing care accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere — with trust, transparency, and compassion.

🔭

Our Vision

To become the leading home healthcare platform in the region, empowering both patients and nursing professionals.
